What is Universal Design?

Universal Design refers to the concept of creating products and environments that are accessible and usable by as many people as possible, regardless of age, ability, or disability. The goal is to ensure that all individuals, no matter the disability, are able to effectivley use .

Understanding Disabilities in Web Design

Web accessibility is vital for individuals with visual, auditory, physical, or cognitive disabilities. It allows them to interact with content on the web in a way that suits their specific needs. By implementing universal design, we can bridge the gap and ensure equal access for everyone.

Principles of Universal Design

  • Equitable Use
  • Flexibility in Use
  • Simplicity and Intuitiveness
  • Perceptible Information
  • Tolerance for Error
  • Low Physical Effort
  • Size and Space for Approach and Use
